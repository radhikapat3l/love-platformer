How to play

  • Move: A / D or ← / →
  • Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Dash: Shift (short burst)
  • Switch character: C (Luna ↔ Rio)
  • Goal: Collect hearts, reach the portal, avoid enemies!
Couple mode: Press C to swap between Luna and Rio. Each has a slightly different feel (jump + dash timing).
