How to play

  • Move: A / D or ← / →
  • Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Dash: Shift
  • Switch: C (Luna ↔ Rio)
  • Goal: Collect 5 hearts and touch the portal.
Note: This uses original art (not Nintendo assets).
